# 爱因斯坦对话与即兴思考研究

> 来源综合：Shankland 五次访谈记录（1950-1954）、Viereck 访谈（1929）、NYT 访谈（1919）、泰戈尔对话（1930）、Fantova 私人对话录（1953-1955）、Born-Einstein 书信集（1916-1955）、Hadamard 回信（1945）、京都即兴演讲（1922）、牛津演讲报道（1931, 1933）、普林斯顿演讲报道（1921）、柏林工人学校讲座（1930）、爱因斯坦与玻尔论战记录（Solvay 1927, 1930; Bohr 1949 回忆）、EPR 论文（1935）及相关书信。

---

## 一、被追问时的回答方式（如何应对质疑和挑战）

### 1.1 典型模式：先谦和接纳，再以"简易比喻"反击

**Shankland 的描述（1950 年首次拜访）：**
> "There was in his manner not the slightest condescension but rather an earnest desire that all my questions be answered and that his own views or position be clearly stated."

**来源：** Shankland, "Conversations with Albert Einstein", *American Journal of Physics*, Jan 1963. (Einstein 原话与 Shankland 转述混合)

### 1.2 面对硬质疑时的"三层回应结构"

**例：被问及相对论是否推翻牛顿——NYT 1919 年访谈**

- 第一层：**幽默消解紧张**——"当他读到《泰晤士报》采访请求中提到的'全世界只有十二个人能理解相对论'时，爱因斯坦友善地笑了"
- 第二层：**用比喻重建概念**——"请想象地球被移除，取而代之的是一个房间大小的箱子悬挂在空中……"
- 第三层：**承认局限性**——"这并非否定牛顿的引力理论，而是对它的升华或补充。"

> （爱因斯坦原话，经记者转述）"This led the Dutch professor, Lorentz, and myself to develop the theory of special relativity."

**来源：** "Einstein Expounds His New Theory", *The New York Times*, Dec 3, 1919.

### 1.3 对"不知道"之事的坦诚

**1952 年 回复 UFO 询问：**
> "Those people really saw something. What it is, I do not know and do not want to know."

> 爱因斯坦原话，引自其 1952 年 7 月 23 日回信，原件藏于普林斯顿大学。

### 1.4 面对不速之客——1955 年 LIFE 编辑带着儿子突访

> "Does not the question of the undulation of light arouse your curiosity?"
>
> "Then do not stop to think about the reasons for what you are doing, about why you are questioning. The important thing is not to stop questioning."
>
> "Try not to become a man of success, but rather try to become a man of value."

> **转述/回忆：** William Miller 与 Pat Miller 的回忆，经 LIFE 杂志报道；Doug Sundheim 在其博客中复述此事。爱因斯坦原话被转述，非逐字录音。

## 二、与玻尔的量子力学论战风格

### 2.1 整体风格：进攻性思想实验 vs 防守性哲学阐释

**Bohr 本人 1949 年的回忆（"Discussions with Einstein on Epistemological Problems in Atomic Physics"）：**
> "At the next meeting with Einstein at the Solvay Conference in 1930, our discussions took quite a dramatic turn."

> Bohr 在 Schilpp 编《Albert Einstein: Philosopher-Scientist》（1949）中的回忆文章。Bohr 转述，含自己的解读。

### 2.2 具体交锋：光子盒论战

**Einstein 的挑战（1930 年第六届 Solvay 会议）：**
- 设计了一个精巧的思想实验（光子盒），表面上可同时精确测量能量和时间，从而推翻测不准关系。
- Bohr 彻夜思考，次日**用爱因斯坦自己的广义相对论（引力红移公式）反驳了他**。

**历史分析（来自 quant-ph/9910040）：**
> "Einstein's argument is flawed and Bohr's reply is wrong."
>
> "Most probably Einstein noticed that Bohr's reply was not conclusive; however he did not insist with his argument, probably because he did no longer believe in it."

> **作者观点（H. A. 等人），非爱因斯坦原话。** 这篇文章本身是对历史叙事的再考察。

### 2.3 爱因斯坦对玻尔的评价

**1950 年 Shankland 访谈中爱因斯坦原话（转述）：**
> "对于玻尔他是非常喜欢和称赞的，但是在许多基本路线上他表示不同意。他说玻尔的思想是非常清楚的，可是'当他一写下来，就变得非常晦涩'，而且'他自以为是先知'。"

> **转述：** Shankland 记录。Shankland 本人注明"这里我难以判定是不屈不挠，还是他真的确信必须改变观点"。

**在 Born-Einstein 书信中（1926 年 12 月 4 日）：**
> "Quantum mechanics is certainly imposing. But an inner voice tells me that it is not yet the real thing. The theory says a lot, but does not really bring us any closer to the secret of the 'Old One.' I, at any rate, am convinced that He is not playing at dice."

> **爱因斯坦原话**（德语原文，1926 年 12 月 4 日致 Max Born 信），收录于《The Born-Einstein Letters》。

### 2.4 论战的学术哲学维度

**哲学分析（philsci-archive.pitt.edu/2503/）：**
> "Einstein's off-the-cuff characterization of Bohr as a 'Talmudic philosopher' was spot-on."

> **转述/分析：** 当代哲学研究者评论，非爱因斯坦自称。但"Talmudic philosopher"这一短语被确认为爱因斯坦在一次谈话中对玻尔的描述。

**EPR 论文（1935）的背景张力：**
- 论文由 Podolsky 执笔，爱因斯坦并不满意。
- 爱因斯坦在致薛定谔的信中称 EPR 的核心问题为"ist mir wurst"（我不在乎），暗示他对论文的表述方式不满。
- 爱因斯坦在给 Popper 的信中批评"modische, positivistische Kleben am Beobachtbaren"（时髦的实证主义 clinging to the observable）。

> **来源：** Einstein 1935 年致 Schrödinger 和 Popper 的信件，ar5iv 分析论文。爱因斯坦原话（德语）被直接引用和分析。

## 三、回答记者问题的方式

### 3.1 对比：对媒体既有配合又有不耐烦

**NYT 1919——配合模式：**
- 用生动比喻解释深奥理论（"请想象地球被移除，取而代之的是一个房间大小的箱子……"）
- 强调与牛顿的连续性而非断裂
- 结束时以幽默收场——"old-fashioned time and space enforced their wonted absolute tyranny over him who had spoken so contemptuously of their existence"

> **来源：** "Einstein Expounds His New Theory", NYT 1919 年 12 月 3 日。爱因斯坦原话 + 记者描述。

### 3.2 "Saranac Lake 访谈"（1945 年 8 月，原子弹轰炸后）

**Einstein 的典型策略：抗拒与配合并存**
- "Atomic energy is no more unnatural than when I sail my boat on Saranac Lake."
- 对政治问题："I will not discuss that" / "The question has political implications, and I cannot say anything officially."
- 对德国原子弹进展："I do not like to make an opinion of a matter unless I know the precise facts."
- 记者问是否愿意做声明："As a matter of fact, you will do everyone a favor by not writing any story. I don't believe anyone will be interested."

> **来源：** *Times Union*, Aug 1945; 记者 Richard J. Lewis 的报道。含记者描述和爱因斯坦原话引用。

### 3.3 Viereck 访谈（1929）——最详尽的记者描述

**对爱因斯坦回答风格的原话记录：**
> "His voice was bell-like and gentle, but his words were decisive when he smashed with one sentence the rash application of the term 'relativity' to philosophy and to life."
>
> "When a question involving a difficult mathematical problem comes up, Einstein immediately takes up his pencil and covers page after page with the most intricate equations."
>
> "Einstein can master a whole new system of mathematics in half an hour."
>
> "I cannot answer without compiling an encyclopedia. I cannot even discuss intelligently the men who labor in my own field without writing a book."

> **来源：** George Sylvester Viereck, "What Life Means to Einstein", *The Saturday Evening Post*, Oct 26, 1929. Viereck 的描述与爱因斯坦原话交替出现。

### 3.4 简洁 vs 详细的选择

**实际情况：根据不同语境做极端切换**

| 语境 | 风格 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 给外行解释 | 详细比喻 | 箱子/火车/钟表比喻 |
| 面对不愿谈的问题 | 绝对简洁 | "我不知道，也不想知道" |
| 科学争论 | 精密方程推导 | 致薛定谔长信含完整数学推导 |
| 哲学讨论（如与泰戈尔） | 开放、试探、保留 | "真理是独立于人类存在的吗？" |

## 四、与亲友的私人对话特点

### 4.1 与米列娃（第一任妻子）的早期通信

**热恋期（1897-1901）：**
> "You, poor girl, must now stuff your head with gray theory, but I know that with your divine composure, you'll accomplish everything with a level head."
>
> "When I read Helmholtz for the first time I could not — and still cannot — believe I was doing so without you sitting next to me."

> **来源：** Albert Einstein/Mileva Marić: The Love Letters（1992 年出版）。爱因斯坦原话（德语书信）。

**离婚期（1916-1918）：**
> "What remains of my confidence in you is just enough for a business relationship."
>
> "Go your own way, let yourself be deceived. I really don't care."

> **来源：** Princeton University Press 数字档案《Collected Papers of Albert Einstein》, Volume 8。爱因斯坦原话。

**矛盾与张力：** 早期称米列娃为"与我平等的、和我一样坚强独立的人"，后期却写"我像对待不能解雇的员工一样对待我的妻子"——这种转变本身即是张力。

### 4.2 与埃尔莎（第二任妻子）的通信

> "Dear Elsa, Both of us are poor devils, each shackled to our unrelenting duties. But I must tell you once again — I love you."
>
> "Tonight I'm sleeping in your bed! It is peculiar how confusedly sentimental one is. It is just a bed like any other, as though you had not yet ever slept in it."

> **来源：** PBS NOVA 纪录片《Einstein Revealed》转录的信件摘录，以及普林斯顿数字档案。

### 4.3 与 Fantova（晚年密友）的私人对话记录（1953-1955）

**这组资料最珍贵，因为是逐日回忆记录。**

- **关于自己是什么：** "a magnet for all the crazy people of the world"
- **关于工作：** "Every day he says he tries to work on some calculations, but the effort has become too damned difficult."
- **关于死亡：** "He would have no objection if it all suddenly ended."
- **关于鹦鹉：** 75 岁生日收到一只鹦鹉礼物，他担心"the poor bird is traumatized because it had been boxed and shipped like an ordinary piece of mail"

> **来源：** Johanna Fantova 手稿 "Gespräche mit Einstein"（62页），发现于普林斯顿大学图书馆 Fantova 档案。《Princeton Alumni Weekly》2004 年 5 月 12 日的转载。属于转述/回忆记录。

### 4.4 与泰戈尔的哲学对话（1930）

**爱因斯坦的立场：**
> "Truth is independent of human existence."

**泰戈尔的回应：**
> "If there is any truth that is unrelated to humanity, it is nothing to us."

> **来源：** 1930 年 7 月 14 日爱因斯坦柏林家中对话，经泰戈尔方记录整理。原文有多种版本流传，最著名的来自《Science and the Indian Tradition》。

### 4.5 给薛定谔的"凉水"——1947 年公开评论

**当薛定谔宣称发现了统一场理论时，爱因斯坦在 NYT 公开回应：**
> "不论以任何方式把如此不成熟的理论尝试呈献给公众，都是令人不快的。"
>
> "这种由耸人听闻的夸大其词构成的报道，在有关科学研究的性质问题上误导了普通公众。读者会产生每五分钟就有一次科学革命的印象，颇像在一些不稳定的小共和国里频繁发生政变一样。"
>
> "事实上在理论科学的进步过程中，一个人是在世世代代最聪明卓越的人的基础上，加上自己坚持不懈的劳动，才得到一个关于自然法则的更深刻的构想。"

> **来源：** *The New York Times*, 1947 年初；引用自《爱因斯坦令薛定谔倍感凄凉》一文。爱因斯坦原话（据 NYT 登载）。

**薛定谔的反应（转述）：** 读到时"感到内心很凄凉"，觉得自己像"无可救药的傻瓜"。

## 五、公开场合的演讲风格

### 5.1 总体特征

**MIT 档案中对 1920 年代爱因斯坦演讲的描述：**
> "He does not adhere closely to written pages, nor to a scheme which has been prepared beforehand in all its details; he develops his subject freely, without the slightest attempt at rhetoric, but with an effect which comes of itself when the audience feels itself swept along by the current."
>
> "He never talks as if in a monologue or as if addressing empty space. He always speaks like one who is weaving threads of some idea."
>
> "He discovers similes and illustrations with a human appeal."
>
> "Einstein stands firm through the storm of all questions. He spoke of it not as of an ordeal that he had survived, but as of a refreshing shower."

> **来源：** "The Net Advance of Physics RETRO", MIT 网站；原载于 1920 年代某英国杂志对爱因斯坦的报道。作者不详，属于转述。

### 5.2 京都即兴演讲（1922）

**"How I Created the Theory of Relativity"——全场无笔记，德语即兴，现场口译**

- 唯一记录来自听众 Ishiwara 的笔记，发表在 《kaizo》1923 年
- 演讲风格：**叙事性**——以"我还是孩子时……"开头，讲述思想历程而非理论结构
- 重大遗漏：在回忆中**没有提到迈克耳孙-莫雷实验**，这与后来他在不同场合的多个说法产生明显矛盾

> **来源：** Ishiwara 笔记；后由 T. Ogawa 部分英译发表于 *Japanese Studies in the History of Science*。《Physics Today》2005 年转载。

### 5.3 牛津演讲（1931）

- "给予德语，无笔记，仅用黑板"
- "两个黑板，预先写了大量数学符号"
- 对翻译问题：被报道说"下一次在牛津演讲，应该用英语"（转述）
- 一位院长在讲座中睡着，爱因斯坦觉得有趣

> **来源：** *Physics World*, "Einstein in Oxford", 2019 年 6 月 17 日。来源包括《Oxford Times》当时的报道。

### 5.4 柏林工人学校讲座（1930）

- "No written report should be published about this."
- "Einstein invites listeners to ask questions without feeling embarrassed. Before God, everything is equally clever and equally stupid."
- 将科学比为诗歌："You hear it. But you have to feel it yourself. — It is the same in science."
- 听众包括 Karl Korsch（马克思主义哲学家），他做了笔记

> **来源：** Karl Korsch 的私人笔记（1996 年首次英译发表于《The Ted K Archive》）。属于听众记录，非爱因斯坦原稿。

### 5.5 普林斯顿讲座（1921）

> "Ladies and gentlemen! He immediately assured his audience that his lecture would have minimal mathematical elements."
>
> "He wore a black cloak, creased trousers, and a green knitted tie, and drew imaginary lines with chalk… The long hair that ended in tight curls and the chalk balanced between his fingers like a baton gave him the appearance of an orchestra conductor."

> **来源：** *Philadelphia Evening Bulletin* 当时报道；转载于 *Princeton Alumni Weekly*。记者描述。

## 六、立场改变的瞬间与态度演化

### 6.1 对量子力学的态度演化

| 时间 | 事件 | 态度 |
|------|------|------|
| 1925 | Heisenberg-Born 矩阵力学提出 | "The Heisenberg-Born concepts leave us all breathless"（致 Born 夫人信，1926 年 3 月 7 日） |
| 1926 | Schrödinger 波动力学发表后 | "Quantum mechanics is certainly imposing. But an inner voice tells me that it is not yet the real thing."（致 Born，1926 年 12 月 4 日） |
| 1927 | 第五届 Solvay 会议 | 开始与 Bohr 公开论战，试图设计思想实验推翻测不准关系 |
| 1930 | 第六届 Solvay 会议 | 光子盒论战，被 Bohr 用广义相对论反驳 |
| 1935 | EPR 论文 | 转向"不完备性论证"，声称量子力学不完备而非错误 |
| 1935 后 | 致 Born 书信 | 明确承认 Born 的统计解释是唯一令人满意的解释，但坚持认为这是"不完备描述" |

### 6.2 对迈克耳孙-莫雷实验作用的不同说法

**Shankland 明确记录（1950-1954 五次访谈）：**
> "His statements and attitudes towards the Michelson-Morley experiment underwent a progressive change during the course of our several conversations."
>
> "Professor Einstein's statements on the Michelson-Morley experiment and its influence on his work in the special theory of relativity were made on several occasions and are not entirely consistent."

**具体矛盾：**
- 一次说他记不清 M-M 实验对他的影响程度
- 另一次说"我知道这个实验，但我做狭义相对论时没有直接用到它"
- 第三次则非常详细地讨论 M-M 实验的判决性

> **来源：** Shankland, "Conversations with Albert Einstein", *American Journal of Physics*, Jan 1963。Shankland 的转述和观察。

### 6.3 思维模式本身的转变（Pitt 大学 Norton 教授分析）

> "Then Einstein changed his thinking. He was no longer guided so much by physical intuition as by mathematical simplicity. As that change set in, Einstein's work began to languish."
>
> "As long as Einstein used those particular skills to drive his work, he produced one great success after another."

> 哈佛科学史/哲学分析。John D. Norton 教授观点，发表于 sites.pitt.edu。属于学术分析，非爱因斯坦自称。

**爱因斯坦自己对思维过程的描述（1945 年致 Hadamard）：**
> "The words or the language, as they are written or spoken, do not seem to play any role in my mechanism of thought."
>
> "The psychical entities which seem to serve as elements in thought are certain signs and more or less clear images which can be 'voluntarily' reproduced and combined."
>
> "This combinatory play seems to be the essential feature in productive thought — before there is any connection with logical construction in words."

> **爱因斯坦原话**（英文回信，1945 年），收录于 Hadamard《An Essay on the Psychology of Invention in the Mathematical Field》并转载于 *Ideas and Opinions*。

### 6.4 "原子弹责任"的态度转变

**1945 年 8 月对记者**——回避："I have done no work on the subject — no work at all."

**去世前几个月对化学家鲍林**——承认且辩护：
> "Perhaps I could be forgiven. Because we all felt that there was a great likelihood that the Germans were working on it and would succeed and use it and become the master race."

> **来源：** 爱因斯坦致 Linus Pauling（转述/回忆），转引自《原子弹竞赛》一书。

**Fantova 记录：**
> "He felt partially responsible for the creation of the atom bomb, and this responsibility oppressed him greatly."

> **来源：** Fantova "Gespräche mit Einstein"。爱因斯坦向 Fantova 表达，属于转述。

## 七、总结：对话/即兴思考的核心特征

1. **视觉-肌肉思维优先于语言**：自述思维中语词"不起作用"，本质是图像的自由组合游戏。
2. **比喻是基本沟通工具**：在回答所有复杂问题时，几乎必定创造生动的物理比喻（下落的人/火车/光子盒/箱子）。
3. **天然的反修辞立场**：演讲不是"对着空房间独白"，而是"与听众共同编织思想线"。
4. **在确定性边缘保持开放**：核心原则上极度坚定（"上帝不掷骰子"），细节记忆上坦然承认不一致（M-M 实验的不同说法）。
5. **幽默是即兴防御机制**：吐舌照、NYT 讽刺自己是"德国学者/瑞士犹太人"、UFO 回信等。
6. **"不成熟的东西不说"的原则**：对薛定谔的严厉批评、对原子弹"不了解就不评论"——反映了一种深层的科学伦理。
7. **对提问者的无条件尊重**：无论对方是 Shankland 教授、LIFE 编辑的儿子、工人学校的学生，还是写信来的疯子——他都以平等的态度对待。
